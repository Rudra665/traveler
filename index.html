Get To Know Us

Explore All Tour Of The World With Us.

We're Aleph Campeur, an adventure based travel organization that promotes responsible travel. We want to show you the world in a new light; one that inspires you to care for it with compassion. Aleph is an emotion evolved from a love for the environment, adventure, people, and a desire to leverage our enthusiasm and travels on behalf of a greater cause. We're revolutionizing responsible and sustainable tourism by building relationships with individuals, communities, governments, NGOs as well as travelers.

Aleph Campeur focuses on providing responsible and sustainable travel experiences. Our sustainable travel methodology draws from the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals and the experience of our partner ecosystem, in addition to research into best practices for responsible tourism. Aleph Campeur helps travellers understand where we live, where we travel to and why it matters. We work with stakeholders to build relationships, share knowledge and drive change towards sustainable travel narratives.

Our Vision And Mission.

At Aleph Campeur, we're on a mission to delight travelers with access to authentic responsible travel as we deliver impeccable service with unparalleled knowledge and keeping a check on rapid carbon emission. We also offer adventure learning as a youth development strategy that fosters physical, emotional, social, and mental well-being through outdoor experiences. Our goal is to inspire new narratives about various communities, art & culture, and less explored destinations for those striving for meaningful, responsible and sustainable travel.
