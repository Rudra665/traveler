Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty and serene landscapes, making it a captivating tourist destination. Nestled in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent, this region offers a diverse range of attractions that draw travelers from around the world. The picturesque Dal Lake, surrounded by snow-capped mountains and dotted with houseboats and Shikaras, provides a unique and tranquil experience. Visitors can indulge in a leisurely ride on the lake while admiring the stunning views of the surrounding Mughal gardens.
In addition to its scenic allure, Kashmir boasts a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its vibrant markets, traditional handicrafts, and distinctive cuisine. The bustling city of Srinagar, with its historic landmarks like the Jamia Masjid and Shankaracharya Temple, offers a blend of modernity and tradition. The region is also known for its exquisite handwoven Pashmina shawls, intricate Kashmiri carpets, and ornate papier-mâché artifacts, providing visitors with unique souvenirs to cherish.
For adventure enthusiasts, Kashmir offers a myriad of outdoor activities, including trekking in the mesmerizing Himalayan ranges, exploring the pristine meadows of Gulmarg, and experiencing the thrill of river rafting in the Lidder River. The scenic landscapes and the pleasant climate during the summer months make Kashmir an ideal destination for those seeking tranquility, adventure, and a cultural immersion in one of the most enchanting places in the world.

Overview

Originating from the city of the Dal Lake or Srinagar, this 04-day tour will take you through other gorgeous places like Srinagar, Pahalgam, and Gulmarg. Join this tour and get acquainted with all the mystique beauty of Kashmir and its gorgeous hill stations!!!

Quick Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive srinagar

On arrival Srinagar meet for assistance and transfer to Srinagar hotel, check in to hotel, after that proceed to visit of world-famous Mughal Gardens visiting the Nishat Bagh (The garden of pleasure) and Shalimar Bagh (Abode of love), Shankarachariya Shiv Temple, Pari Mahal (Fairies adobe) and Ancient City tour of Srinagar. Evening return to hotel. Dinner and overnight stay in srinagar hotel..

DAY 2: SRINAGAR - SONMARG 02 HRS DRIVE (98 KMS) FROM SRINAGAR

After having breakfast, you will be driven for full day excursion trip to Sonamarg. Sonamarg is a place of enthralling beauty, lies in Sindh Valley, streamed with flowers, surrounded by mountains, and perched at an altitude of 2690 m above sea level. Visited less frequently by tourists, it has as its backdrop, snowy mountains against a clear sky. It is a quiet alpine retreat surrounded with sycamore, silver birch, fir and pine trees and the last halt on the Kashmir side for the drive from Srinagar to Leh. It is also the base for some interesting treks to the high altitude of Himalayan Lake. (IN SONAMARG, Pony Rides must be paid directly). Evening return to Srinagar hotel for Dinner and overnight stay at hotel

DAY 3: SRINAGAR TO GULMARG (APPROX 50 KM / 01 HRS 30 MIN DRIVE)

After breakfast, proceed for a picturesque drive to Gulmarg. Gulmarg was discovered as a tourist destination by the British in the 19th century. Prior to that, Mughal emperors vacationed in the Gulmarg valley which is about 03 Kms long and up-to 01 km wide. It is exquisitely situated in a pine surrounded basin of the Pir Panjal range at an altitude of 2,730 meters above sea level and one of the most famous tourist destinations of Kashmir. It also has one of the world’s highest green golf courses with 18 holes, as well as a clubhouse, which is a historical building. It is one of the most beautiful summer resorts in the valley. It is also popular for its golf course during summer season and as a ski resort during the winter season, on arrival proceed for a picturesque ride of worldfamous Gondola chair car (pay directly ticket charges) later visit famous Shiv mandir in Gulmarg. Evening return to srinagar at hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

DAY 4: SRINAGAR TO PAHALGAM (100KM/02-03 HRS DRIVE)

After breakfast, check out from hotel, you will be driven from Srinagar to Pahalgam, en-route visit Saffron fields of Pampore, see the beautiful countryside, lots of rice fields and Awantipora ruins on the way. On arrival in Pahalgam, check in to hotel, after freshening up enjoy into Pahalgam enjoy the surrounding beauty or walk to Baisaran, which is about 2 km from Pahalgam. A small village set in pine and fir forest is pleasing and offers good view. Check in to hotel. Dinner and overnight stay at hotel. (IN PAHALGAM, PONY RIDES AND JEEPS TO BE HIRED FOR CHANDANWARI OR ARU VALLEY, MUST BE PAID DIRECTLY).

DAY 5: PAHALGAM TO SRINAGAR (100 KM / 02-03 HRS DRIVE)

After breakfast, check out from hotel and transfer to Srinagar houseboat check in to houseboat, later ride on a Shikara ONE HOUR, - a Kashmiri gondola - to the watery vegetable markets and see the mountains mirrored in the lakes. You will get a view of some remarkably interesting places around the Lake. In the evening return to houseboat. Dinner and overnight stay at houseboat.

DAY 6: SRINAGAR AIRPORT DEPARTURE

After breakfast check out from houseboat and you will be transferred to Srinagar airport on time to board flight. TOUR ENDS BUT SWEET MEMORIES ALWAYS REMAINS.
