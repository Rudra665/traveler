Kerela

(5 Days & 4 Nights)

Experience the enchanting beauty of Kerala with our specially crafted 5 Days and 4 Nights itinerary that promises a perfect blend of nature, culture, and relaxation. Begin your journey in the picturesque city of Kochi, where the rich history unfolds through its colonial architecture and vibrant markets. Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry as you witness a Kathakali dance performance and explore the ancient Jewish Synagogue.
Venture into the serene backwaters of Alleppey aboard a traditional houseboat, where lush green landscapes and tranquil waters create an idyllic setting. Savor delicious Kerala cuisine as you cruise through palm-fringed canals, offering a unique perspective of local life. The journey continues to Munnar, a hill station renowned for its tea plantations and mist-covered mountains. Enjoy breathtaking views, visit tea estates, and embark on nature walks to truly connect with the region's pristine beauty.
The next leg of your adventure takes you to Thekkady, known for its wildlife sanctuary and spice plantations. Engage in a thrilling boat safari in Periyar National Park, where you might spot elephants, tigers, and various species of birds. Additionally, explore the aromatic spice gardens to learn about Kerala's spice trade history.
Conclude your Kerala sojourn in the coastal town of Kovalam, famed for its golden beaches and relaxed ambiance. Spend your days unwinding by the sea, indulging in Ayurvedic spa treatments, or experiencing the vibrant local culture. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a history buff, or seeking tranquility by the ocean, our 5 Days and 4 Nights in Kerala offer an immersive experience that captures the essence of this captivating destination.

Quick Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Kochi, sightseeing in Kochi

Pick up from Kochi Airport / Ernakulam railway station and transfer to the hotel. Afternoon with local sightseeing in and around Fort Kochi. Visit Dutch Palace [closed on Friday & Saturday], Jewish Synagogue [closed on Friday & Saturday], Jewish Street, St. Francis Church and Chinese Fishing Nets. Overnight at the hotel in Cochin. Overnight in a hotel.

DAY 2: Kochi-Munnar (140 km, appx. 4 to 5 hrs.)

After breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Munnar. Enjoy the scenic drive and reach Munnar. Check in at the hotel. Later go for short sightseeing in Munnar town. Overnight in hotel

DAY 3: Sightseeing in Munnar

After breakfast go for local sightseeing. Visit Rose Garden, Photo Point, Honey Tree, Mattupetty Dam, Echo point, Tea Museum. Overnight in a hotel.

DAY 4: Munnar- Alleppey (170 km, appx. 5 hrs.)

After breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Thekkady. Upon arrival check in at the hotel. Later visit elephant camp and spice plantation. Overnight in a hotel.
Optional:-on Direct payment basis.

  • Kathakali and Kalaripayuttu show
  • Jeep ride in the hills

DAY 5: Munnar- Alleppey (170 km, appx. 5 hrs.)

Early in the morning enjoy the boat ride in Lake Periyar and see the Periyar wildlife reserve. You could spot Check out and proceed to Kochi airport/Ernakulam railway station for your onward journey.
Trip ends here.
