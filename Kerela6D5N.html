Kerela

(6 Days & 5 Nights)

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," offers a captivating and diverse experience for travelers during a 6-day and 5-night stay. Nestled in the southwestern part of India, Kerala is renowned for its lush green landscapes, serene backwaters, and pristine beaches. The journey begins with a visit to the enchanting city of Kochi, where the blend of historic architecture and modernity creates a unique ambiance. Explore the historic Fort Kochi, with its colonial-era buildings, vibrant markets, and the iconic Chinese fishing nets that line the coast. Delve into the rich cultural heritage of the region by witnessing a Kathakali dance performance, a traditional art form that brings ancient stories to life with elaborate costumes and expressive gestures.

Moving on, a journey through the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey aboard a traditional houseboat provides an unparalleled experience. Surrounded by palm-fringed landscapes and quaint villages, the backwaters showcase a slower pace of life. Enjoying local cuisine prepared on the houseboat adds a delightful culinary dimension to the journey. The adventure then takes you to Munnar, a hill station known for its sprawling tea plantations and breathtaking views. Explore the tea estates, visit the Eravikulam National Park to spot the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, and take in the panoramic vistas of the Western Ghats. The cool climate of Munnar offers a refreshing contrast to the coastal regions, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers.

The final leg of the Kerala journey takes you to the pristine beaches of Varkala or Kovalam, where golden sands meet the Arabian Sea. Relax by the shore, indulge in water activities, and witness stunning sunsets that paint the sky in hues of orange and pink. Ayurvedic massages and wellness treatments, deeply rooted in Kerala's traditions, provide a rejuvenating conclusion to the trip. Kerala's unique blend of nature, culture, and relaxation ensures that every moment of the 6-day and 5-night journey is filled with unforgettable experiences and a sense of connection to the soul of this picturesque destination.

Kerela (6 Days & 5 Nights)

Overview

Kerala the God’s Own Country, there are just too many things to do & experience there, be it the never ending meadows of tea spread around the hills of Munnar or the tranquilizing beauty of the backwaters of the golden sandy beaches of Varkala. Kerala as a state is blessed with geography like no other, it offers everything a travel nut would wish from a vacation. An ideal place to spend the colourful days of a new bound relationship & make memories which last a lifetime.

Quick Itinerary

Day 1: Kochi to Munnar (130 km, appx. 4 to 5 hrs.)

Upon arrival at Ernakulam railway station or Kochi airport, meet your driver and then you will travel to Munnar. Upon arrival in Munnar, check-in to your hotel. Evening is free for relaxation. Overnight at Munnar.

DAY 2: Sightseeing in Munnar

After breakfast go for sightseeing in and around Munnar. You will visit the beautiful Mattupetty lake, echo point and top station to enjoy the scenery. Later see the rose garden and tea museum. Evening is free for shopping. Overnight at Munnar.

DAY 3: Munnar to Thekkady (112 km, appx. 3 to 4 hrs.)

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Thekkady. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel. Later visit the spice plantation or select from our optional activities. In the evening, enjoy kathakali and kalaripayattu shows. Overnight at Thekkady.
Optional tour – Make your own tea program in tea estate on direct payment basis.

DAY 4: Thekkady to Alleppey (110 km, appx. 3 hrs.)

Enjoy the boat ride in Lake Periyar and see the Periyar wildlife reserve. Check out from the hotel and proceed to Alleppey. Upon arrival check in to your hotel. In the late afternoon we will take you for a sunset boat ride in Vembanad lake. Overnight at Alleppey.

DAY 5: Alleppey to Kovalam (165 km, appx. 4 hrs)

After breakfast, Hotel and drive to Kovalam; on arrival check-in into the hotel. Kovalam is one of the prime beach destinations in India. Enjoy the evening watching a spectacular sunset or swimming at the picturesque Kovalam Beach. Overnight is to be spent at the hotel.
Trip ends here.

DAY 6: Half day Trivandrum tour

Morning visit to Trivandrum. See Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Raja Ravi Verma art gallery. Free time to shop . at the local market.
Overnight at Kovalam
