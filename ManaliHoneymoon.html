Manali Honeymoon

Nestled in the heart of the mighty Himalayas, Manali stands as a jewel in the crown of Himachal Pradesh, India. This charming hill station has earned its reputation as a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Manali's scenic beauty is unparalleled, with lush green valleys, meandering rivers, and snow-capped peaks forming a breathtaking backdrop. The Beas River gracefully winds its way through the town, offering not only a soothing view but also opportunities for various water activities like river rafting and trout fishing.
Manali is a gateway to the adventure-packed Rohtang Pass, where travelers can revel in the thrill of snow activities and witness panoramic views that extend as far as the eye can see. The Solang Valley, another gem in Manali's crown, invites visitors to experience the rush of adrenaline with its ski slopes and paragliding adventures. Old Manali adds a touch of bohemian charm to the destination, featuring vibrant cafes, artistic alleys, and the ancient Manu Temple, steeped in mythology.
This Himalayan retreat is not only a feast for the senses but also a cultural and spiritual haven. Manali's diverse offerings, from the tranquility of the Hidimba Devi Temple to the vibrant atmosphere of its markets, create an atmosphere where every visitor can find something to cherish. Whether seeking serenity in the lap of nature or craving an adrenaline-fueled escapade, Manali unfolds a tapestry of experiences that lingers in the memories of those fortunate enough to explore its wonders.

Manali Honeymoon

Overview

A land of splendid mountains, a pristine place which feeds and provides solace to the mind and soul, a pious land which is associated with the supernatural, an enigma of a place which attracts people from every nook and corner of the world- It's incredible, it's beautiful, it’s enchanting- It's Himachal!
Himachal Pradesh is a magical place where the sun sets in paradise. Reorganized as the 18th Indian state in 1971, Himachal is located in the north-western region of the Himalaya. Himachal is landlocked between Jammu and Kashmir to the north, Tibetan plateau to the west, and Punjab to the east; the state stands apart in terms of its sheer topographic diversity and breathtaking pristine natural beauty. It is said that the land possesses such magnetism that it attracts nature lovers and voyagers from not only India but across the globe

Quick Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive at Delhi & Overnight journey to manali

In the evening at 5 pm, Pick up from Delhi Volvo / Tempo Point and drive towards Manali. Overnight Journey

DAY 2: Hadimba devi temple, Manu Temple, Vashisht temple & Mall road

On arrival check in at hotel and after freshen up proceed for local sightseeing including the 450 years old Hidimba Devi Temple, the oldest temple in Manali, Manu temple and then Vashisht Temple famous for hot water water spring. In the evening you can enjoy the mall road, later back to the hotel. Enjoy your dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel. overnight stay at a hotel at Manali.

DAY 3: Solang valley, Atal tunnel & Sishu

After Early morning Breakfast, proceed to Solang Valley ( Snow Point ) with Atal Tunnel Situated at 13 km northwest of Manali, particularly a pictorial spot presenting awesome scenery of snow clad mountains and glaciers. The most common sports at Solang Valley are parachuting, paragliding, skating and zorbing. Dinner at Hotel, Overnight Stay.

DAY 4: MANIKARAN AND KASOL

After breakfast check out from the hotel and visit the Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara at Manikaran after that drop at Manali Bus stand for onwards journey To Delhi.

DAY 5: REACH DELHI

Reach Delhi early in the morning taking home memories which last forever..
